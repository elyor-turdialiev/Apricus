Apricus began with a single English class and a stubborn belief: that every learner deserves a tutor who knows their name, their goals, and their pace. Three years later, that belief has only grown stronger.

Our Story

From one classroom to seven subjects.

Apricus Online School was founded in 2022 with a clear vision — to make language learning effective, personalized, and results-driven. What started as a focused English teaching practice has grown into a multi-disciplinary platform trusted by students with diverse goals.

As our students' needs evolved, we expanded from English to German, Spanish, Latvian, Russian, and Portuguese — along with Business English and Mathematics — helping learners achieve real academic, professional, and communication results.

Today, Apricus represents a modern approach to education, where flexibility, personalization, and measurable progress come first. We don't just teach — we deliver real progress.

What we stand for

Six principles that shape every lesson, every learning plan, and every relationship we build with our students.

/ 01

Personalized learning

Every plan is tailored to your level, goals, and pace. We don't believe in one-size-fits-all curricula — we believe in lessons that fit you, and only you.

/ 02

Real results

Confidence in speaking, fluency in writing, mastery of practical skills. We focus on what you'll actually use — and we measure progress, not effort alone.

/ 03

Proven + modern

Strong fundamentals meet interactive materials. We honor what works in language pedagogy while embracing what's new and effective.

/ 04

All-in-one platform

Languages, Business English, exam prep, Mathematics — under one consistent standard. One tutor, multiple goals, seamless coordination.

/ 05

Flexible format

Learn anytime, anywhere. Online doesn't mean impersonal — it means accessible. Your classroom is wherever you have a screen and a moment.

/ 06

Ongoing support

Continuous guidance, homework help, and feedback between lessons. Your tutor isn't just a teacher — they're a partner in your journey.

Our journey

From a single classroom in 2022 to a multi-subject platform serving learners across continents.

2022

The beginning

Apricus is founded with a focused mission: deliver high-quality, personalized English instruction. The first cohort of students includes future teachers, IELTS candidates, and professionals seeking fluency.

2023

Beyond English

Driven by student demand, we expand into Latvian, Russian, Spanish, and German. The Online Speaking Club launches, giving students a low-pressure space to practice and build confidence.

2024

New disciplines

Mathematics joins the roster — a natural extension for students seeking academic support across subjects. Business English programs deepen with industry simulations and academic writing.

2025

Portuguese & beyond

Portuguese is added to the language portfolio. The Personal Semester Assistant program launches, offering full academic support throughout the school year.

2026

Today, and tomorrow

Apricus serves students across Europe, Central Asia, and beyond — with the same commitment to personalization that launched the school. The mission remains: through difficulties to the stars.
