About Apricus

An online school built around the student.

Apricus Online School was founded in 2022 with a simple belief: every learner deserves a tutor who knows their name, their goals, and their pace.

Our story

From a single classroom to seven subjects.

Apricus started as a focused English teaching practice. The first cohort included future teachers, IELTS candidates, and professionals seeking fluency for their careers.

As students' needs evolved, we expanded — first to other languages (Latvian, Russian, Spanish, German, Portuguese), then to mathematics, then to specialized programs like Business English and exam preparation.

Today, Apricus serves students across Europe, Central Asia, and beyond. The mission hasn't changed: deliver real, measurable progress through truly personalized instruction.

Our values

What we stand for

Six principles that shape every lesson, every learning plan, and every relationship we build with students.

01

Personalized learning

Every plan is tailored to your level, goals, and pace. No one-size-fits-all curricula.

02

Real, measurable results

Confidence in speaking, fluency in writing, mastery of practical skills — and a way to measure them.

03

Proven + modern methods

Strong fundamentals meet interactive materials. We honor what works while embracing what's new.

04

One platform, many paths

Languages, Business English, exam prep, and mathematics — all under one consistent standard.

05

Flexible format

Online, on your schedule. Your classroom is wherever you have a screen and a moment.

06

Ongoing support

Continuous guidance and feedback between lessons. Your tutor is a partner, not just a teacher.

Our journey

Three years of growth

From a single classroom in 2022 to a multi-subject school serving learners across continents.

2022

The beginning

Apricus is founded with a focused mission: high-quality, personalized English instruction. The first students included future teachers, IELTS candidates, and working professionals.

2023

Beyond English

Driven by student demand, we expand into Latvian, Russian, Spanish, and German. The Online Speaking Club launches.

2024

New disciplines

Mathematics joins the roster as a natural extension for academic support. Business English deepens with industry simulations and writing modules.

2025

Portuguese & Semester Assistant

Portuguese is added. The Personal Semester Assistant program launches for full-school-year academic support.

2026

Today

Apricus serves students across Europe, Central Asia, and beyond — with the same commitment to personalization that launched the school.
