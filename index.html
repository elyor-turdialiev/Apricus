Why Apricus?

From Latin apricus — "warmed by the sun." We believe learning, like sunlight, should reach every learner directly and personally.

/ 01

Personalized by design

Every learning plan is tailored to your level, goals, and pace. No cohorts, no fixed curricula — just lessons that fit you.

/ 02

Real, measurable progress

Mock exams, regular consultations, and progress tracking ensure you don't just learn — you advance, visibly and consistently.

/ 03

Flexible, everywhere

Online and on your schedule. Whether you're in Riga, Tashkent, or anywhere in between — your classroom is wherever you are.

/ 04

Proven + modern methods

Strong fundamentals meet interactive materials. We honor what works while embracing what's new in language pedagogy.

/ 05

One platform, many paths

Languages, Business English, exam prep, and Mathematics — all under one roof, with consistent quality across every subject.

/ 06

Ongoing support

Continuous guidance, homework help, and feedback between lessons. Your tutor isn't just a teacher — they're a partner.
