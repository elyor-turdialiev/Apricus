Every lesson is dedicated entirely to you. No cohorts, no shared attention, no rigid pace.

Meet your teacher

Hi, I'm Elyor.
Founder of Apricus.

"Teaching isn't about transmitting information. It's about lighting a fire — and walking alongside the student as they tend it."

I started Apricus in 2022 with a focused English teaching practice and a simple belief: every learner deserves a tutor who knows their name, their goals, and their pace.

Three years later, the school has grown into a multi-subject platform — but the principle hasn't changed. Each student gets a personal plan, direct access to their teacher, and lessons designed around what they actually need.
